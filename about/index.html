About Us

Lucky Bay Groups LTD is the result of unwavering dedication, years of real estate experience, and unwavering passion. It's no secret that we live in a period when unconventional thinking reigns supreme and Nigerians' housing demands continue to rise.

Lucky Bay Groups was founded to address Nigeria's housing shortage by creating sustainable housing with high-quality building materials in the best and most serene setting at affordable pricing. Our enthusiasm, integrity, transparency, resilience, excellent construction team of specialists, and the fact that we spare no expense in ensuring that we build sustainable housing with quality materials have all led to the growth of Lucky Bay Groups.
